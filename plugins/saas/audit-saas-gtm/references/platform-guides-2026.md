<product_hunt research_date="Q1/2026">

**Verified 2026 statistics:**
- 26.5% of launches attract 500-1,000 users
- 22.4% gain 1,000-2,000 users
- 50% of founders report some increase in registrations
- 30% experience significant increase
- 56% rated experience as "very satisfied"
- 49% fail by relying on Product Hunt alone

**Expected ROI:** 5,000 visits → 100 trials (2%) → 20 customers (20%) = $10K ARR at $500 ACV.
Conversion rates vary 20-50% based on ICP fit.

**2026 challenge:** Manual homepage featuring has gotten "ruthlessly selective".

**Pre-launch (2-4 weeks):**
- Build supporter network: target 400-500+ commitments
- Export contacts from LinkedIn, email, communities
- Send 50-100 personalized DMs/day for 2 weeks (video DMs: near 100% open rate)
- Target: ~1,000 upvotes for Product of the Day
- Need 20-50 upvotes/comments in first hour for algorithm trigger

**Launch assets:**
- Thumbnail: 240×240px
- Gallery: 4-6 images at 1270×760px
- Tagline: max 60 characters, benefit-focused
- First comment: your story + ask for feedback
- Video: optional but recommended (1-2 min demo)

**Launch day:**
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Respond to every comment within 1 hour
- Never ask for upvotes (against TOS)
</product_hunt>

<linkedin research_date="Q1/2026">

**Best performing formats for B2B:**
1. Personal journey/story posts
2. "X things I learned building Y"
3. Behind-the-scenes photos
4. Customer success stories
5. Industry insights/hot takes

**Posting cadence:** 3-5/week during launch, 8-10 AM local time.
**Tips:** Hook in first line, short paragraphs, no links in body (put in comments).
</linkedin>

<x_twitter research_date="Q1/2026">

**Content mix:**
- 40% building in public
- 30% industry insights/value
- 20% product updates
- 10% direct promotion

**Thread strategy for launch:**
1. Hook: What you launched and why
2. Problem: The pain point
3. Solution: How your product solves it
4. Features: 3-4 key capabilities
5. Social proof: Early results
6. CTA: Link to try
7. Ask: Retweet request
</x_twitter>

<communities research_date="Q1/2026">

**Reddit:**
- Be a community member first
- Provide value without promoting
- Format: "I built [X] to solve [Y] — looking for feedback"
- Subreddits: r/SaaS, r/startups, r/Entrepreneur, r/SideProject

**Hacker News (Show HN):**
- Title: `Show HN: [Name] – [One-line description]`
- Post during US work hours (Tue-Thu best)
- Be ready for technical questions, accept criticism gracefully

**Indie Hackers:**
- Transparency about numbers
- Share failures, not just wins
- Revenue tracking connected
</communities>

<review_platforms research_date="Q1/2026">

**G2 and Capterra (B2B):**
- Complete profile: description, features, pricing, screenshots, demo video
- Ask happy customers directly for reviews (provide direct link)
- Respond to all reviews
- Monitor competitor listings
- Use relevant category keywords
</review_platforms>
