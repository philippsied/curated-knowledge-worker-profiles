<market_trends research_date="Q1/2026">

**Verified 2026 trends:**
- 79 companies in PricingSaaS 500 Index now offer credit models (126% YoY increase)
- 38% of SaaS companies use usage-based pricing (2025 data)
- 61% of SaaS companies had some form of usage-based model by 2022
- Gartner projected 30%+ of enterprise SaaS solutions would incorporate outcome-based components by 2025

**2026 pendulum swing:**
While usage-based grew in 2025, there is a swing back toward simplicity in 2026.
Some providers reintroduce seat-based packages or tighten caps on usage-based
plans to make costs more predictable.

**Model distribution (2025 data, latest verified):**

| Model | Adoption | Benchmark |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Per-User | 57% (declining) | Median $45/month |
| Hybrid (Per-User + Usage) | 61% (growing) | +34% LTV/CAC |
| Usage-Based elements | 43% | +8 PP vs. 2024 |
| Freemium | 38% | 8-12% conversion to paid |
| Flat-Rate | 14% (strongly declining) | -25% YoY |

**Key fact:** 1% pricing improvement = 12.7% more profit (strongest lever).
78% of SaaS use value-based pricing.

<pricing_models research_date="Q1/2026">

**1. Outcome-based pricing** — charge for results, not access.
- Intercom Fin AI: $0.99 per successful resolution (launched 2023)
- Riskified: charges only for approved fraud-free transactions
- ServiceNow: piloting outcome-based with guaranteed efficiency improvements
- HubSpot Breeze: moving to credits-based with performance tiers

**2. Usage-based pricing** — charge for consumption.
Examples: API calls (Stripe, Twilio), compute (Vercel, AWS), storage (Cloudflare R2), tokens (OpenAI, Anthropic)

**3. Hybrid pricing (recommended for most)**
```
Base platform fee (predictable revenue)
  + Usage component (scales with success)
  + Value capture (% of outcomes)
```

**Tier design principles:**
- 3-4 tiers: Free/Trial → Starter → Pro/Growth → Enterprise
- Differentiate by: limits, features, service level, compliance
- Annual contract incentive: 15-20% discount standard
</pricing_models>

<pricing_page_best_practices research_date="Q1/2026">

**Must-haves:**
- Clear tier comparison table
- Prominent CTA per tier
- Annual/monthly toggle
- Enterprise "Contact Sales" option
- FAQ section

**Trust signals:**
- Customer logos
- "No credit card required" for trials
- Money-back guarantee
- Security certifications

**Red flags in pricing strategy:**
- Per-seat only with no usage component
- No free trial or freemium tier
- Hidden pricing requiring sales call (for SMB)
- Single tier with no upgrade path
- Pricing that does not scale with customer success
</pricing_page_best_practices>

<freemium_tradeoffs research_date="Q1/2026">
- 140% higher visitor-to-signup conversion than free trials
- 30-50% higher support costs per paying customer
- 86% of SaaS >100M USD use ≥3 pricing dimensions
- Annual contracts reduce churn by factor 3-5
</freemium_tradeoffs>
